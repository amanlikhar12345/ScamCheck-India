Stay Safe Online - Essential Tips

Red Flags to Watch For

  • Urgent action required immediately
  • Requests for personal information
  • Poor grammar and spelling
  • Too-good-to-be-true offers
  • Suspicious links or attachments

Protection Strategies

  • Verify sender identity independently
  • Never share OTP or passwords
  • Contact banks directly for verification
  • Be skeptical of unsolicited messages
  • Report suspicious activity

Emergency Contacts

  • Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930
  • National Helpline: 155260
  • Your Bank's Customer Care
  • Local Police Cyber Cell

Quick Safety Tips

  • When in doubt, don't click or respond
  • Banks never ask for details via SMS
  • Verify job offers through official channels
  • Be cautious with social media links
  • Trust your instincts
